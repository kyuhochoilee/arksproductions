Take your ensembles online.

ARKS Productions offers high-end mixing, mastering, and video editing that can bring your performances to the screen.

Our Process

Recording

We'll walk you through recording audio at home without professional equipment, and provide free cloud storage for your group to upload to.

Audio Editing

We'll process, tune, and fix the rhythms of each part and put it together into a mixed and mastered recording using professional software and equipment.

Video Editing

We'll then edit together every video and synchronize it to the recording using industry-standard software to create a clean, high-quality video performance.

Publishing

We'll send the finished video to you to share with the world!
